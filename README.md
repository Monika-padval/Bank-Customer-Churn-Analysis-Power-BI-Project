# 🏦 Bank Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard
An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes customer churn patterns in the banking sector. The project identifies high-risk customer segments based on age, activity status, credit score, tenure, product holdings, and geography, helping drive data-driven customer retention strategies.
*****
## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn patterns in a banking environment using Power BI. The objective is to identify the key factors influencing customer attrition and provide actionable insights that can help improve customer retention strategies.
****
## ⚠️ Business Problem
The bank was experiencing customer churn but lacked clear visibility into which customers were most likely to leave. Management needed a data-driven approach to understand churn behavior, identify high-risk customer segments, and support retention initiatives to reduce customer loss.
****
## 🎯 Project Objective
The dashboard analyzes customer churn across multiple dimensions, including age group, gender, geography, active status, credit score, tenure, account balance, and product holdings. The goal is to uncover churn trends, identify at-risk customers, and support data-driven decision-making.
****
## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Visualization
****
## ✨Key Features
- Interactive KPI Cards
- Customer Churn Analysis by Age Group
- Churn Analysis by Gender
- Credit Score vs Churn Analysis
- Active vs Inactive Customer Comparison
- Product Ownership vs Churn Analysis
- Tenure-Based Churn Analysis
- Average Balance Comparison
- Geographic Churn Distribution Map
- Dynamic Filters and Slicers
****
## 💡Key Insights
The overall customer churn rate is approximately 20%.
Senior customers exhibit higher churn rates compared to younger customers.
Inactive customers are significantly more likely to leave the bank.
Customers with lower credit scores tend to have higher churn rates.
Certain product segments show elevated churn behavior.
Churn patterns vary across different geographic regions.
****
## 🚀 Business Impact
The insights generated from this dashboard help identify high-risk customer segments and support targeted retention strategies. By focusing on customers most likely to churn, the bank can improve customer engagement, reduce attrition, and enhance overall customer lifetime value.
****
## 📊 Dashboard
The dashboard provides an interactive and user-friendly interface that enables stakeholders to monitor churn trends, explore customer segments, and make informed business decisions.
